Sanborn Research LLM Corpus Proof of Concept

Archival corpus PDFs/images/transcripts → structured source metadata → deterministic counts/entity extraction → LLM-assisted thematic bins → candidate hubs/corridors → human verification → GIS/story map layer.

This prototype stages the workflow for turning Buffalo, Pittsburgh, and possible intercity corridor evidence into structured research leads. It does not replace archival verification; it organizes and scores evidence so the team can review stronger leads faster.
